Political Scorecard

The Political Scorecard allows various user groups to analyze and contribute to the political landscape through three distinct scoring methods: Competitive, Policy, and Solution scoring.

Policy Scoring

Primarily for legal professionals and those interested in making legal assertions and associations between statements and proposed solutions.

Competitive Scoring

For average citizens to debate and analyze issues using simplified terms and frameworks created by expert democratic systems.

Solution Scoring

Designed for experts in fields like science, engineering, and economics to propose and vote on comparative flow charts of alternative solutions.
